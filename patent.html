Abstract :

A system 100 and method for money withdrawal from an automated teller machine 102 include a camera 104, a finger print sensor 106, and a first server 108 in communication with one or more other servers 120 under one or more protocols in a network 130. The first server 108 include one or more processor to feed with accounts details by a person to validate for a first time, capture facial images and at least one finger print to validate the person for a second time using artificial intelligence. Further, generate a warning message on a registered mobile device 140 held by the customer when data is not matched, else; send onetime password and feed to validate the person for a third time and allow to proceed with transections, and record all transactions. The one or more other servers 120 include servers of Unique Iidentification Authority of India (UIDAI), National Payment Corporation of India (NPCI).
